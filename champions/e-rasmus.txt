e-rasmus (TANK)
Character Description:
E-rasmus is a modern version of a defensive hero. Balancing physical and magical resistance with damage reflection, he can withstand long battles.

Skills:

Rock+ – Increases both physical and magical resistance. (+5 to resistances, 2 uses)

Thorns – Reflects a larger portion of damage back to the enemy. (30% damage return, 8 uses)
