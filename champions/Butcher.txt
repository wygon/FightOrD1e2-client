Butcher (LIFESTEALER)
Character Description:
Butcher is a ruthless fighter who drains life from his victims. The longer the battle, the stronger he becomes thanks to passive healing while attacking.

Skills:

Ad boost – Increases physical damage to finish enemies faster. (+10 AD, 3 uses)

LifeSteal – Passive – Each attack restores a small amount of health until the end of the fight. (20 HP per attack, always active – code: 111)
