The Rock (TANK)
Character Description:
The Rock is a classic tank whose goal is to survive as long as possible and reflect received damage. Relentless and tough, he’s perfect for the front line.

Skills:

Rock+ – Temporarily increases physical resistance. (+5 Physical Resist, 2 uses)

Thorns – Passive – Reflects part of the received damage back to the attacker. (15% damage return, always active – code: 112)
