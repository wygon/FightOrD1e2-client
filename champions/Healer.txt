Healer (MAGE)
Character Description:
Healer is a support mage with great healing potential, yet surprisingly effective offensively. He has the ability to maintain a safe distance from enemies.

Skills:

Spark! – Summons lightning to strike the enemy. (70 Magic Damage, 7 uses)

Dodge – Avoids the enemy and increases range. (+1 range, 3 uses)

Revitalize – Restores health. (+70 HP, 8 uses)
