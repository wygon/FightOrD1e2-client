Luxia (MAGE)
Character Description:
Luxia is a powerful ranged sorceress specializing in crushing magical attacks and healing. Her dark magic is unmatched in offensive combat.

Skills:

Magic ball – Sends a magical orb dealing high magic damage. (120 Magic Damage, 2 uses)

Dark power – Dark magic that deals massive damage to the enemy. (250 Magic Damage, 1 use)

Revitalize – Heals a portion of health. (+80 HP, 3 uses)
