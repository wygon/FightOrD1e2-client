Zoe (MAGE)
Character Description:
Zoe is an unusual melee mage whose style is based on quick magical strikes and manipulating enemy position through sleep and dodges.

Skills:

Boom! – Deals powerful magical damage. (120 Magic Damage, 2 uses)

Dodge – Avoids the enemy, increasing range. (+1 range, 1 use)

Sleep – Puts the enemy to sleep and moves away, increasing distance. (+1 range, 3 uses)
