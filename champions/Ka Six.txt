Ka Six (ASSASSIN)
Character Description:
Ka Six is a master of quick kills and evasion. He can deal massive damage in an instant and escape when the situation becomes dangerous.

Skills:

Slash – A powerful slash that deals high physical damage. (150 AD damage, 1 use)

Seeds – Throws projectiles that deal moderate physical damage. (80 AD damage, 2 uses)

Jump out – Moves away from the enemy, gaining 1 point of range. (+1 range, 1 use)
