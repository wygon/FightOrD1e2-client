Poisoner (FIGHTER)
Character Description:
Poisoner is a tactical melee fighter who combines physical and magical power. He specializes in poison and distance control.

Skills:

Poison – Poisons the enemy, dealing damage until the end of the battle. (50 damage over time, 2 uses)

Run – Moves away from the enemy, becoming a ranged character for 1 turn. (+1 range, 3 uses)
