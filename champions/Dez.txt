Dez (ASSASSIN)
Character Description:
Dez is a ruthless melee assassin who specializes in fast, double strikes and silent kills. He gives opponents no time to react.

Skills:

Double attack – Allows two strikes instead of one in a single action. (2 attacks, 2 uses)

Throw shuriken – Throws a shuriken at the enemy, dealing high physical damage from a distance. (100 AD damage, 2 uses)
