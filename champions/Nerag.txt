Nerag (FIGHTER)
Character Description:
Nerag is a brutal melee fighter who relies on powerful attacks and enhanced resistance. His blows are doubly deadly.

Skills:

Ad boost – Temporarily boosts attack strength. (+10 AD, 2 uses)

Power fists – Delivers two hits instead of one in a single action. (x2 hits, 2 uses)

TANKYYYY – Increases resistances for better survivability. (+5 to both resistances, 3 uses)
