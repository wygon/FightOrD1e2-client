Suirad (FIGHTER)
Character Description:
Suirad is a durable melee fighter who weakens opponents through bleeding while increasing his own damage resistance.

Skills:

Ad boost – Increases physical damage to dominate the fight. (+10 AD, 2 uses)

Bleeding – Applies a bleeding effect lasting until the end of the battle. (50 damage over time, 3 uses)

TANKYYYY – Increases physical and magical resistance. (+5 to both resistances, 3 uses)
